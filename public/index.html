How Does It Work?

We have email. We have post mail. Why not combine both? Pen Pal Post makes it possible for you to send digital letters to your friends with a delay similar to post mail.

Step 1: Basic Details

Go to the Send a Letter page and fill out the form with some basic details about yourself and the letter recipient.

Step 2: Write Your Letter

Add your message to the form!

Step 3: Submit & Wait!

Submit the form and you're done! You will received an estimated delivery time and your friend will recieve a link to their mailbox to see your letter!
